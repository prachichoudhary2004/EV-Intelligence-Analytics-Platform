# Product Roadmap - EV Market Intelligence Platform

## Phase 1: Foundation (COMPLETED)
- [x] Medallion Data Architecture (Bronze/Silver/Gold).
- [x] Spark ETL Engine (Simulated).
- [x] Semantic KPI Layer & Narrative Engine.
- [x] Premium Streamlit UI with Glassmorphism.
- [x] XGBoost & Prophet Forecasting models.
- [x] Data Quality Monitoring & Health Scoring.

## Phase 2: Scale & Connectivity (Q3 2026)
- [ ] **Live Snowflake Integration**: Replace local Parquet files with a live Snowflake/Databricks cloud connection.
- [ ] **Advanced NLP Interface**: Integrate LLM (GPT-4/Claude) for "Chat with your Data" functionality.
- [ ] **Automated Anomaly Alerts**: Email/Slack integration for real-time market surge/drop alerts.
- [ ] **User Authentication (SSO)**: Full role-based access control (RBAC) with Auth0.

## Phase 3: Global Expansion (Q1 2027)
- [ ] **International Data Support**: Expand datasets to include EU, China, and Emerging Markets.
- [ ] **Supply Chain Analytics**: Integrate raw material (Lithium, Cobalt) price tracking and battery supply chain risk scoring.
- [ ] **Carbon Credit Simulation**: Add modules for tracking and forecasting regulatory carbon credits.
